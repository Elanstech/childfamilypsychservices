About

With over 10 years of experience working with children, adolescents, adults, and families, Dr. Doheny specializes in person-centered therapy which enables her to create individualized treatment for each client. She believes that while we can't change difficult situations of the past, we can work together to better understand and resolve challenges in your life.

Specializations

  • Person-Centered Therapy
  • Play Therapy & Sandplay Therapy
  • Expressive Therapy
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Neurofeedback & Biofeedback

Assessment Services

Psychological evaluations offered for:

  • ADHD Assessment
  • Learning Disabilities
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ADOS-2)
  • Developmental Delays

"I believe that every individual or family is unique and the treatment should be catered to their needs. By applying complementary therapy approaches and techniques, we will work together to help you achieve a more fulfilling and meaningful life."
